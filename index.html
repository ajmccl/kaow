About Us

We are a family owned restaurant specializing in Thai cuisine. In addition, we also have a sushi bar. Most Fridays we have live music featuring local artists performing a variety of genres. Catering events and banquets are also available upon request.

We are open for dine-in, takeout, and delivery(through apps like DoorDash or GrubHub).

Our daily hours are from 11AM until 9PM.
